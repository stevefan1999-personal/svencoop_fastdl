============PROJECT: GUILTY - Mission 1===================

Objective Info: 

You are a Special Operations Executive (SOE) of the United States special forces
      
You have been captured along with your team by a corrupt marine force. Your 
mission was to prevent the base transmitting secret information in regards to 
SOE operations.
Transmission of this information is imminent, make sure you destroy the satellite
communication link before proceeding.

-Primary Objective  : Escape out of corrupt marine complex.

-Secondary Objective: Disable satellite link to stop the communication       

Tips:

» There are secrets in some maps keep your eyes open.

» Keep each other healed or you will not survive your escape.
