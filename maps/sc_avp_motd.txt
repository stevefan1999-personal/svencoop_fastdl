Story - 

After being cut off from the rest of the world, your general has ordered you to get to, and kill, the empress alien.

Your team has been supplied with an APC, though it’s under fire, from the world's resistance group, that has commandeered a chopper. The resistance also has some of its militia armed with 50 Cal Chain Guns.


Objectives -

Get through the starting zone, and make your way to where the alien empress is held in one of the Warehouse's.

Kill the empress, and her minions for the FTL Chopper.
